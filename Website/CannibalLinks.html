Related Sites and Resources

For a good technical description of dynamical friction and tidal stripping, see sections 7.1 and 7.3 of Galactic Dynamics, by James Binney and Scott Tremaine.

For more sophisticated models of satellite galaxies, orbital decay, and tidal stripping, see websites by

    Chris Mihos (CWRU): http://burro.astr.cwru.edu/models/models.html
    Kathryn Johnston (Wesleyan): http://www.astro.wesleyan.edu/~kvj/

For a description of satellite galaxies in the local group, see

    Dwarf Galaxies of the Local Group, by Mario Mateo, Annual Reviews of Astronomy and Astrophysics, 36, 435, 1998.

For discussions about how the accretion of satellites might build up the halo of the Milky Way, see websites by

    Amina Helmi (Leiden): http://www.strw.leidenuniv.nl/~ahelmi/research.html

For more galaxy merging Java fun, go to our Galaxy Crash JavaLab
